About us

Built by clinicians, for clinicians.

CarePractice was created to give health and wellness professionals a simple, secure way to manage their work—without feeling like they have to be full-time administrators.

Our mission

We believe that when practitioners spend less time on paperwork and platforms, they can spend more time with people. Our mission is to make the “business side” of care as effortless as possible.

  • Support clinicians with thoughtfully designed tools.
  • Protect sensitive client data with strong security practices.
  • Help small practices operate with the power of large systems.

What we value

  • Privacy first. Built on secure infrastructure and best practices.
  • Clinical insight. Features guided by real-world feedback.
  • Accessibility. Simple to start, flexible as you grow.
  • Human support. Help when you need it, from real people.
